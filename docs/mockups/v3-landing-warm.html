Questions?

How does the AI score essays?
WriteRight uses GPT-4o aligned to Singapore's MOE 1184 marking scheme. Essays are scored on Task Fulfilment (10) and Language (20) for a total of 30. Feedback references your actual paragraphs.
Is it really aligned to MOE standards?
Yes. Our rubric follows MOE 1184 Situational Writing and Continuous Writing criteria ‚Äî Content, Language, Organisation, and Tone. The same dimensions your school uses.
What levels does it support?
Sec 1 through Sec 5 (O-Level track). JC GP support coming soon.
Is my child's data safe?
All data encrypted, never shared or sold. Essays are processed for scoring only ‚Äî not used to train AI models.
Can my child upload handwritten work?
Yes. Snap a photo and our OCR transcribes it. Review and edit before submitting for scoring.
How is this different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT gives generic feedback. WriteRight scores against MOE rubrics with band scores, tracks progress over time, provides structured model rewrites, and has parent dashboards with student gamification. Built specifically for SG English essays.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. One click in settings. No lock-in, no penalty. Free tier continues after cancellation.
