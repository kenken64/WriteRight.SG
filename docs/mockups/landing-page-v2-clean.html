Frequently Asked Questions

Is WriteRight SG officially endorsed by MOE?
While WriteRight SG is not directly endorsed by MOE, our assessment criteria are carefully aligned with MOE English Language syllabus standards and PSLE/O-Level marking schemes to ensure relevance and accuracy.
Is my child's data safe and secure?
Yes, we take data privacy seriously. All essays and personal information are encrypted and stored securely. We never share student data with third parties and comply with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).
Does WriteRight replace traditional tuition?
WriteRight complements traditional learning methods. While our AI provides instant, detailed feedback for practice, we recommend it as a supplement to classroom learning rather than a complete replacement for human instruction.
What happens after my 7-day free trial ends?
Your account automatically switches to our Free plan (3 essays/month) unless you choose to continue with Plus. No credit card required for the trial, and you can upgrade or cancel at any time.
How does the family rewards system work?
Parents can create custom rewards linked to specific writing achievements (like completing essays or reaching certain band scores). Students add wishes to their list, parents approve and link them to goals, and rewards become claimable when goals are met.
Can I use WriteRight for other subjects?
Currently, WriteRight focuses specifically on English essay writing. We're considering expansion to other subjects based on user feedback and demand.
