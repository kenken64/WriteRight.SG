Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI score essays?
WriteRight uses GPT-4o fine-tuned on Singapore's MOE 1184 English Language marking scheme. Essays are scored on Task Fulfilment (10 marks) and Language (20 marks) for a total of 30. Feedback references specific paragraphs from your essay.
Is it really aligned to MOE standards?
Yes. Our scoring rubric is modelled directly on the MOE 1184 Situational Writing and Continuous Writing criteria used in O-Level English. We score Content, Language, Organisation, and Tone/Register ‚Äî the same dimensions your school uses.
What levels does it support?
Currently Sec 1 through Sec 5 (O-Level track). We're working on JC (A-Level GP) support ‚Äî join the waitlist to be notified.
Is my child's data safe?
Absolutely. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share or sell student data. Essays are processed by AI for scoring only and are not used to train models.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, cancel with one click from your account settings. No lock-in, no penalty. Your free tier access continues after cancellation.
Can my child upload handwritten essays?
Yes! Take a photo of handwritten work and our OCR (optical character recognition) will transcribe it. You can review and edit the transcription before submitting for grading.
How is this different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT gives generic writing feedback. WriteRight scores specifically against Singapore's MOE rubric with band scores, tracks progress over time, provides structured model rewrites, and has parent dashboards + student gamification. It's purpose-built for SG English essays.
